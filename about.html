Our History

Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) began in 1956 as Dewan Latihan RIDA, offering skills training for rural development. It evolved into MARA College in 1965 and later became ITM (Institut Teknologi MARA) in 1967, expanding its academic programs. UiTM gained university status in 1999, growing into Malaysia's largest public university, with campuses nationwide and a focus on empowering Bumiputera students.
