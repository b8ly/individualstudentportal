UiTM Student Portal

Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM), Malaysia's largest public university, was established in 1956. With campuses nationwide, it offers diverse programs
and fosters innovation, shaping skilled graduates for national and global contributions.

visit us to know more!

Courses We Offer

UiTM offers diverse courses in disciplines like Engineering, Information Sciences, and Health Sciences, combining academic excellence with practical experience. Supported by expert faculty and modern facilities, our programs prepare students to thrive in the global workforce.

Pra Diploma

UiTM's Pra-Diploma program prepares students for diploma studies by strengthening their academic foundation.

Diploma

A UiTM diploma provides practical and theoretical knowledge in specific fields, completed in 2-3 years with coursework and hands-on training.

Degree

UiTM offers 3-4 year undergraduate programs in fields like engineering, IT, health sciences, and education, blending theory with practical skills.
